Welcome

Gabriella Plants is an online storefront specializing in indoor foliage.

Meet Shane - General Manager

Founded 24 years ago, Gabriella Growers has become a staple in the argriculture industry in Central Florida. Family Owned and operated, the one acre facility focuses on high quality indoor house plants mainly pothos, philodendron and boston ferns. Over the past two years, the greenhouse has expanded from wholesale to our online storefront, Gabriella Plants. We strive to bring the quality and care of our family run business to each plant we ship.
